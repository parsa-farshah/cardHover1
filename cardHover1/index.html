Drake

Regarded as one of the greatest rappers of all time, he is credited with popularizing R&B sensibilities in hip-hop music. Drake first gained recognition by starring as Jimmy Brooks in the CTV teen drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation (2001–2008) and began his music career by releasing the mixtapes Room for Improvement (2006), Comeback Season (2007), and So Far Gone (2009).

Hiphopologist

Soroush Valizadeh professionally known as “Hiphopologist” or “Dr. RapFa”, is a Persian rapper and lyricist hailing from Babolsar, Mazandaran in Iran. Blending sharp lyricism with a distinct underground edge, he rose to prominence with standout tracks like “Ghors” and “Nabz”, which gained recognition across the Persian hip-hop scene.

Tataloo

Amir Hossein Maghsoudloo (Persian: امیرحسین مقصود لو; born 21 September 1987), known professionally as Amir Tataloo (Persian: امیر تتلو), is an Iranian singer, rapper and songwriter.[3] He is one of Iran's most famous and controversial artists known for his full body tattoo and his frequent open dialogue toward politics and social aspects of Iran's younger generations. He is part of the first generation of the Iranian underground hip hop scene and also one of the first R&B singers in Persian.[4]
